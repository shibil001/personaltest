SUNNIYYA CHENNAMANGALLUR

Sunniyya Arabic College is situated at Chennamangallur village located in Kozhikode district. It was established in 1980 and got affiliation in 1995. The college has a congenial atmosphere for academic pursuint and aims at providing education to the local people. The village Chennamangallur, which is situated 25kms East of the district head quarters, has a dense population of minority community.
The Sunni Education Trust, which runs the institution, aims at the uplift of educationally backward classes. The college spreads over an area of 5.5 acres at the bank of the river 'Eruvazhinhi' and it provides a serene atmosphere for the students and faculty.

ABOUT COLLAGE

Currently Sunniyya Arabic Collage offers UG, PG, PhD, and certificate programmes in various fields such as Language, Education, Vocation and more.

Our Vision

The college provides students with quality education and support services. The institution creates awareness among the students regarding their ultimate responsibilities towards family, institution, society, surroundings and private and public resources and makes them capable of competing with the shifting trends of modern education on behalf of the community, the society, the nation and the world

Our Mission

The college fosters academic and career success through the all-round development and training of students making them competent to carry out various duties of nation-building, and to develop critical thinking, effective communication, creativity, and cultural awareness in a safe, accessible and affordable learning environment to empower them with positive qualities and qualifications.

History

Sunniyya Arabic College is an aided and affiliated post graduate college established in 1979 at Chennamangallur, Mukkam, Calicut, Kerala. It is managed by the Chennamangallur.
